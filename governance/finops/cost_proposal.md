# Migration Cost Proposal & Breakdown

## Estimated Project Costs
Based on the **WTPS-Cost-Break-down-Proposal**, the following estimates apply to the 5,000-user scope.

### 1. Professional Services
- **Design & Architecture**: $45,000
- **Migration Execution (Wave 1)**: $120,000
- **Training & Change Management**: $30,000

### 2. Infrastructure (GCP Consumption)
- **GWM Worker Nodes (10 nodes @ n1-standard-8)**: ~$1,500/month
- **Database (MySQL/CouchDB)**: ~$400/month
- **Storage (GCS & PD-SSD)**: ~$800/month

### 3. Licensing
- **Google Workspace Enterprise Plus**: $[Contract Price] per user/month.
- **Migration Tooling**: Included in Workspace entitlement or standalone GWM cost.

## ROI & Savings
- **M365 E3/E5 License Reduction**: Projected 45% reduction in seat count.
- **Support Consolidation**: Simplified management of tech units via Google Cloud Identity.
